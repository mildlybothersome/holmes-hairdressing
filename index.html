Meet Your Stylist

Hello, I'm Eviekate Holmes, owner and stylist of Holmes Hairdressing.

I am a fully trained hair stylist that can do everything from a cut and blow, all the way to the perfect hair you need for proms and weddings.

I am a SMP (Scalp Micropigmentation, also known as hair tattoo) technician, which is a treatment of small pigment dots to emphasis and improve hairlines.
